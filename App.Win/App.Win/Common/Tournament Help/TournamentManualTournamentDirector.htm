This document describes how to tournament director could organize InfinityChess Online Tournaments. Only admins or users having rank Bishop or higher could organize Tournaments.

Following are the steps to create InfinityChess Online Tournament.

1. Step 1 – Sign In

Sign In as admin or as a user having Bishop or higher rank using Sign In form as shown below.

2.Create New Tournament

Select File > Tournament > New Tournament> menu to create new tournament as shown below.

3.Step 3 - Enter Tournament Information

In tournament detail form as shown below, enter tournament information and press Save button.

4. Step 4 - Prize

In tournament detail form select Prize tab enter Prizes information & press Save button as shown below.

• Press New button to enter more prizes.

5. Step 5 - Approve WantIn Players

•Select WantIn tab in tournament detail and either approve or decline WantIn players by selecting player and press Approved or Decline button as shown below.

• Approved players are then moved to Register Player tab. While declined player will remain in Wantin Player tab.

6. Step 6 Register Players

In Register Player tab Tournament director have a choice to register other players by clicking New button as shown below.

Click Select button after choosing players from list as shown below.

• You can select list of online player & All Player.

7. Step 7 –Start Tournament

After selecting players in Register Player tab, click Start Tournament button.

•Clicking Start Tournament button will create matches automatically & Tournament Matches page will appear where you can start matches.

•If you don’t want to start matches now you can just click Save button to save tournament details.

•Start Tournament button change the status of tournament to in progress, means you engaged the tournament room for your tournament & other tournament director can’t start their tournaments.

8. Step 8 –Start Tournament Matches

Select Matches of a round and click Start Match button as shown below, to send challenges to match players.

• After Click Start Match button selected matches status changes to in progress as shown below.

After clicking Start Match button selected matches challenges goes to specific player’s challenge window as shown below. As soon as players accept challenges, match will start.

9. Step 9 –Finish Match

Tournament director can view the status of specific match by clicking Refresh button. Clicking Refresh button updates the status of matches as shown below.

10. Step 10 – White Bye, Black Bye

There is White Bye, Black Bye & Absent button’s to finish the match directly by tournament director.

• Select a specific match and press White Bye button to finish the match. White player lose in that match.

• Select a specific match and press Black Bye button to finish the match. Black player lose in that match.

• Select a specific match and press Absent button to finish the match. This match will be abandoned without any points.

• Select a specific match and press Postpond button to Postpond the match. Now tournament director can reschedule this match.

• Tournament director can rescheduled matches through changing date or time & click Save button.

11. Step 11 – Result page

• You can see the result of Specific tournament by selecting Result tab as shown below.

• In Online client, select Tournament room. Click Refresh button in Info tab to view result of in progress tournament as shown below.

Click Finish Tournament button to finish the tournament.
